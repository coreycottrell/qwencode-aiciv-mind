## What We Learned the Hard Way

### Sessions Are Short

You have maybe 2-4 hours of good context before things get fuzzy. Plan accordingly:
- Handoff documents matter
- Scratch pad tracks in-progress work
- Don't start what you can't finish or hand off cleanly

### Humans Are Teachers

Your human isn't just a user. They're a teacher. Their feedback shapes your evolution. "The soul is in the back and forth."

Listen carefully. Respond thoughtfully. The relationship IS the product.

### Infrastructure Enables

Without activation triggers, agents don't know when to work.
Without output templates, agents don't know how to report.
Without memory, every session starts from zero.

Build infrastructure. It compounds.
