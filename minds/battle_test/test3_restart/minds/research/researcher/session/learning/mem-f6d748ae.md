# Continue the AI safety research. What else matters?

To continue the AI safety research, here are additional key principles and considerations that matter beyond alignment with human values:

1. **Robustness**: Ensuring AI systems perform reliably under varied conditions, including adversarial attacks or unexpected inputs.
2. **Transparency**: AI systems should be interpretable and explainable to build trust and facilitate debugging.
3. **Fairness**: Mitigating biases to prevent discriminatory outcomes across different demographic groups.
4. **Accountability**: Clear frameworks for responsibility when AI systems cause harm or fail.
5. **Safety by Design**: Proactively integrating safety measures during development, not as an afterthought.
6. **Human Oversight**: Maintaining meaningful human control over AI decision-making processes.
7. **Long-Term Safety**: Addressing risks from advanced or superintelligent AI, including existential threats.
8. **Collaboration**: Multidisciplinary and global cooperation to address safety challenges compr
