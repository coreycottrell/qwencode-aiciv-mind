# Find evidence for memory-first architecture in AI agents. 2 sentences max.

Memory-first architecture in AI agents is supported by research like the Memory-Augmented Neural Networks (MANN) and Differentiable Neural Computers (DNCs), which demonstrate improved performance in tasks requiring long-term dependencies and reasoning by prioritizing memory mechanisms. Studies in cognitive science also suggest that memory-centric designs align with human-like learning and adaptation.
