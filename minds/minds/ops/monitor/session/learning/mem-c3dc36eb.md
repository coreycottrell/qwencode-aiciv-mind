# What metrics would tell us if our mind system is actually improving over time? D

To measure the improvement of our mind system over time, we should track the following three metrics:

1. **Task Completion Rate**:
   - **Definition**: The percentage of tasks completed successfully out of the total tasks assigned.
   - **Why it matters**: This metric directly reflects the system's ability to execute tasks effectively. An increasing trend indicates improved performance and reliability.

2. **Response Time**:
   - **Definition**: The average time taken to respond to a task or query from the moment it is assigned.
   - **Why it matters**: Faster response times indicate more efficient processing and better system performance. Tracking this helps identify bottlenecks and areas for optimization.
